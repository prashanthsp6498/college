ABOUT OUR DEPARTMENT



The department came into existence along with the inception of the institute in the year 2016 to teach Mathematics to Engineering students. The department has a team of 4 qualified and experienced faculty members. Faculty members of the department strive to create friendly environment in which students can learn Mathematical skills to solve Engineering and Technological problems.


Vision


To educate the students in various domains of Mathematics including analytical and computational Mathematical skills to solve Engineering and Technological problems having real world applications.


Mission


To inculcate value based education and application oriented training in the field of Mathematics and Engineering Sciences to the students to prove their intrinsic value and enable them to contribute to the over all development of Scientific and Technical Education..
