ABOUT PRINCIPAL



Dr. Y.T. Krishne Gowda recieved his master degree(Masters of Engineering) from Indian Institute of Science(IISc),Banglore,India and received his Ph.D from Indian Institute of Technology(IIT),madras,India.


Dr. Y.T. Krishne Gowda worked in AICTE,new Delhi as Director.During his tenure he worked as member of Executive committe RGPV,Bhopal,where doctorate has been awarded to Dr.A P J Abdul Kalam before he became the president of India.Also he worked as member of Washington Accord.HE is a founder secretary to Maharaja Education Trust(MET) and a mentor and professor of Mechanical Engineering and Management Sciences in Maharaja Institute of Technology,Mysore. HE has 33 years of Teaching Research & Administration experience. hEhas published about 35 papers in national and international journals and presented his papersin India and abroad.


He received the best paper award - Dr.Despande Award,for his research paper in the international conference at IIT, Madras.His research interests are in Fluid Dynamics, LEan Management and Six Sigma methodology and has also received The lifetime Achievement Award - 'Certificate, Bronze Medal and Memento' in view of recognition of his Contributions, Achievements and Exvellence in Mechanical Engineering. HE has also been honored with the institution of Engineers Award. He has sucessfully guided 3 Students for their Ph.D Degree & currently guiding 5 students in cutting edge technology and management.
