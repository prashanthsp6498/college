ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT



MITT has been conducting various Programs on Soft Skills, Life Skills for all its students under the Entrepreneur and Technical Skills Development Program (ETSDP). The objective of this program is to make a student ‘Industry Ready’ and bridge the gap between classroom teaching and Industry needs. The Programs under ETSDP are conducted progressively every week and they are part of the accademic weekly schedule. The Program exposes the students to the various requirements of the ‘Interview Preparedness’. The modules that are covered under the ETSDP are English Language enhancement, Business Communication Skills, Problem Solving skills and Soft Skills – under which the students are facilitated with the sessions on Self Awareness, Self-Motivation, Interpersonal and Intrapersonal Skills, Group Discussions, debates, Interview facing, Etiquettes etc. A team of dedicated faculty exclusively for ETSDP looks after the Programs and schedules every week and progress record of every student is maintained.


Objectives:


  • Create awareness among students regarding available career options and help them in identifying theircareer objectives.
  • Guide the students in developing skills and job-search strategies required to achieve their career objectives.
  • Organize activities concerning career planning.
  • Act as a bridge between students, alumni and employers.
  • Developing the students’ technical knowledge and soft skills to meet the corporate recruitment process.
  • The entrepreneurship development area is also encouraged and monitored with the view of making job creators apart from job seekers.
